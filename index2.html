About Me

My Story

View my resume â†—

My interest in visual design started in first grade, when I won a local coloring contest. Since then I've been obsessed with making things people love--from hand-drawn illustrations to digitally created graphics, logos, websites and animations.

After graduating from Southwestern University, I worked in creative staffing where I helped companies find top-tier design talent. This is where I learned all about the field of UX and product design. In 2015, I began taking classes at the Art Institute of Austin, General Assembly Austin, and in 2017, I completed an immersive UI/UX Design bootcamp.

Since 2017, I've worked at small to mid-size startups as a UI/UX and product designer. I believe that the best designs come from collaboration, and I pride myself on being able to bring people together to create .

When I'm not sketching on my ipad or browsing dribbble for inspiration, you'll find me running at LadyBird lake, swimming laps at Deep Eddy, or sipping on a fromar (frozen margarita) at Matt's El Rancho.
