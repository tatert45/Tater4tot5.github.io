The Challenge

As Director of Business Development on Vitamin T's Austin, Texas team, I was responsible for building brand awareness and expanding business in the Austin market. This occasionally involved designing and illustrating various print and web graphics and collateral to promote the Vitamin T brand and events.

Projects

STUMP ATX

STUMP was a quarterly event we held in order to recruit UX Design and Developer talent. As a team we came up with T-shirt design concepts. I was charged with designing and creating the graphics for event T-shirts and social media that would be on brand with Vitamin T.

Christmas Postcard

In 2015 I was responsible for designing and cretaing our office Christmas card to be sent to all of our clients and talent. Out of several Designs I created, we ended up choosing the christmas lights card.

Top Banana Greeting Cards

In theme with Vitamin T's "Top Banana" campaign, I designed and illustrated greeting cards to be sent to our clients and talent.

Talent Postcards

As part of a talent recruitment campaign, I was responsible for designing postcards featuring an inspirational quote from Steve Jobs.
