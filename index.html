About Me

My Story

VIEW MY RESUME

I began my creative career in the First Grade, when I won a local coloring contest. I've been drawing and designing everything from cards, tshirts, logos, and websites ever since.

After graduating from Southwestern University, I worked in Creative Staffing where I helped Lead Designers, Art Directors and Creative Teams find Digital Creative talent. I learned all about the field of UI/UX and took classes at the Art Institute of Austin and General Assembly and in 2017, I completed an immersive UI Design program.

My passion for illustration, combined with my experience in the digital creative industry, give me the unique ability to create informed and functional designs.

When I'm not sketching on my ipad or watching Bob's Burgers, you'll find me running at LadyBird lake, swimming laps at Deep Eddy, or sipping on a fromar (frozen margarita) at Matt's El Rancho.
